Every moment can have an impact. Everything changes. Nothing is still the same after one second has passed. You can't look at something twice with the same intention or the same meaning. I get my inspiration out of my life, my daily experiences, everything that happens to me, everything that changes my day, life, thinking and overthinking.

Her new work (2015-2016) and projects are sculptures who want to capture storys. The sculptures want to tell you a history/story/question. She’s searching for the perfect combination between texts and image, image and sculpture, sculpture and sculpture to show the best impression of the feelings and the story she wants you to be aware off. You can see that storytelling is something that’s hidden in every work she makes or creates. For her, storytelling, is one of the most important things in the world and in her world, without storys and a little imagination, there’s no world, there’s no life. Only a planet. 


"It's true, I think too much. But it's a source of inspiration that makes me feel alive. Thinking gives me freedom. I can think or dream anything I want. It's my imagination who feeds the inspiration. But my inspiration also feeds my imagination."
